Project 6 — Autobiographical Game

This autobiographical mini-game follows “Joejoe,” my cooking alter-ego, through three scenes: catching ingredients, handling stove mishaps, and finishing an imperfect but personal dish. It turns my beginner cooking journey into playful interactions—balancing mistakes, quick fixes, and a small feeling of pride at the end.
