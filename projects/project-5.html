Project 5 — Optical Illusion

This optical illusion uses many dots arranged along circular paths that continuously change their size, color, and position. These subtle shifts create a strong illusion of motion and depth, making the screen feel alive and almost three-dimensional. I also think this could work well as a live phone wallpaper because of its calm but dynamic visual effect.
