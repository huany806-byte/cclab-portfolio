Project 4 — Exquisite Corpse

This group project is an exquisite-corpse–style collaboration where each team member designed and coded a different part of the body. I created the upper body, giving it a muscular look. Together, our pieces formed a funny character with blue-green skin, strange glasses, a waving arm, a strong upper body, and a short leg.
